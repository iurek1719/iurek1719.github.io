About Villa Inizio

Modern, Villa Inizio (built 2021), 250 m from the sea, 250 m from the beach. Private property: 700 m², swimming pool (7 x 3 m, 27 m², depth 150 cm, seasonal availability: 15 May - 15 Oct) with internal staircase. Outdoor shower, barbecue. In the house: internet access, washing machine. Motor access to the house (50 m via unmade road). Parking (for 3 cars) at the house on the premises. Shop 250 m, restaurant 260 m, pebble beach 250 m, public bathing area 900 m. Marina 4 km. Nearby attractions: Aquapark 2 km, NP Krka 17 km.

Villa Inizio – Brodarica, Croatia

Small, quiet, family-friendly resort with 3'000 inhabitants, in the southern part of the region, directly by the sea, 5 km south from Sibenik, 15 km south-east from Vodice, 19 km north from Primosten. All shops, restaurants etc. in the resort. Indoor aquatic centre, wellness centre, sauna, solarium, massage at 2 - 5 km. More possibilities for shopping and entertainment 2 - 5 km.
